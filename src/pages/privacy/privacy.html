Effective date: {{ effectiveDate }}

Chessifiled (“Chessifiled”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a web application for creating and comparing chess games from PGN notation. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Chessifiled, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

When you use Chessifiled, we may collect the following:

  • Account information. To save your work you sign in using Firebase Authentication with one of: an email address and password, a phone number (verified by SMS), or a Google or Facebook account. Depending on the method you choose, we receive your email address, phone number, and/or basic profile information (such as your name) from the sign-in provider. We never receive your social-account or email password.
  • Content you create. The chess files you create — including PGN text, file and folder names, labels, and the board images generated from your games — are stored so you can access them across sessions and devices.
  • Usage and device data. We use analytics to understand how the app is used. This may include device and browser type, approximate location derived from your IP address, pages viewed, and interactions. This information is used in aggregate to improve the service.
  • Local storage. We store limited data in your browser (such as your theme preference and sign-in session) so the app functions correctly.

How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and keep you signed in.
  • To save, sync, and display your chess files and generated board images.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, abuse, or security incidents.
  • To communicate with you about your account or the service when necessary.

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-party services

Chessifiled relies on the following third-party services, which process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase / Google Cloud — Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, and Analytics. Your account and content are stored on Google Cloud infrastructure. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Meta (Facebook Login) — if you choose to sign in with Facebook, Meta processes your login and shares basic profile information with us. See Meta’s Privacy Policy.

These providers process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored using Google Cloud services. We rely on the security measures those services provide and take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data retention

We retain your account information and content for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Generated board images are derived from chess positions and are stored by the position they depict, not by user identity. You may request deletion of your account and associated content at any time (see “Your choices and rights”).

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can:

  • Delete individual files and folders within the app at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

We will respond to reasonable requests in accordance with applicable law.

Children’s privacy

Chessifiled is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

International users

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country, including in the United States, where data-protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.
